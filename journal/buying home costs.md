## The Valencian Community ÔÇť5% downÔÇŁ scheme and what it actually is

- Historically, IVF communications around launch emphasised **up to 95% financing** (using a guarantee sized to bridge from 80% to 95%). ţłÇciteţłéturn22search19ţłü  
- The **current call documentation** describes a structure that can reach higher (up to 100% of reference value) via a larger guarantee tranche (up to 20%). ţłÇciteţłéturn4view0ţłéturn1view0ţłü

### Does using the scheme change the interest rate (95% LTV pricing)?
